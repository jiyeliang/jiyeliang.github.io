Biography

Ming Li is a Distinguished Professor at the Department of Educational Technology, and the Department of Computer Science, with Zhejiang Normal University. Poior to this, he finished two postdoctoral fellowship from the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, La Trobe University, Australia, and the School of IT in Education, South China Normal University, China. Ming received his Ph.D degree in Computer Science from La Trobe University, Australia. His research interests include machine learning, educational data mining, specialized in graph neural networks, learning theory for neural nets, randomized learning techniques. etc.
I am looking for highly-motivated students to work with me on the exciting area of machine learning, applied mathematics, computational intelligence, etc.

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning: Fundamentals and Algorithms for Deep Neural Networks
  • Graph Neural Networks: Theory, Models, Algorithms and Applications
  • Applied and Computational Harmonic Analysis: Wavelets, Scatterd Data Modelling on Manifold
  • Recommendation System, Educational Data Analytics, Knowledge Tracing, Sentiment Analysis for E-learners
