Biography

I am a Professor at the Department of Educational Technology of Zhejiang Normal University. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Information Technology in La Trobe University in 2017, master degree in Applied Mathematics in China Jiliang University in 2013, bachelor degree in Information and Computing Sciences in Shandong Normal University in 2010. From 2017-2019, I have completed two postdoctor positions in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of La Trobe University, and the Department of Educational Information Technology of South China Normal Univeristy, respectively. I also have been a visiting scholar at The University of New South Wales (working with Dr. Yu Guang Wang) from July to September 2019.
I am looking for highly-motivated students to work with me on the exciting area of machine learning, applied mathematics, computational intelligence, etc.

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning: Fundamentals and Algorithms for Deep Neural Networks
  • Graph (Convolutional) Neural Networks: Graph Classification, Node Classification;
  • Applied and Computational Harmonic Analysis: Spherical Harmonic Analysis, Wavelets, Scatterd Data Modelling on Spheres.
  • Recommendation System, Educational Data Analytics, Knowledge Tracing, Sentiment Analysis for E-learners;
  • Randomized Learning Techniques for Deep Neural Networks
